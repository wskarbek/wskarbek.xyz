Why this website looks like it's from 1995?

I miss times when websites were simple, full of colorfull GIFs and created solely by the individuals who owned them. Since this website is exclusively mine, I wanted to give it unique touch that sets it apart from the more contemporary designs. Additionally, given its size and scope as small personal site, there was no need for me to use massive dynamic frameworks like Angular or React. By using simpler approach, the site remains compact and fast, with a current size of just 595 KB. Of course, my other projects do utilize more modern frameworks.
